About Veganholic

Veganholic is an online restaurant selling its own prepared vegan starters, dishes, accompaniments, desserts and beverages. It was created by four university friends: Giulia Invercini, Ninh Nguyen, Linh Pham and Eva Solliez. The team wanted to make affordable, diverse and delicious vegan food that consumers could easily find and order.
